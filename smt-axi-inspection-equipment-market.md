<p><p>The SMT AXI (Automated X-ray Inspection) equipment market comprises two main types: inline and offline systems. Inline AXI systems are integrated into the production line, providing real-time inspection of assembled PCBs for immediate feedback and adjustments, enhancing efficiency. Offline AXI systems, on the other hand, are standalone units used for batch inspections after production, allowing for detailed analysis of products without disrupting the manufacturing process. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the quality of electronic assemblies.</p></p>
