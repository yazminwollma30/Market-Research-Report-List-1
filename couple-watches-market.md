Automatic watches are powered by the movement of the wearer's wrist, utilizing a rotor that winds the mainspring without needing manual intervention. In contrast, manual mechanical watches require the wearer to wind the crown periodically to store energy for timekeeping. Both types often appeal to couples seeking matching styles, combining elegance with intricate craftsmanship, making them popular choices for shared moments and sentimental gifts.</p></p>
