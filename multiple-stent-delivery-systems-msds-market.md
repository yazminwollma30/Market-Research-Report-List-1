<p><p>Multiple Stent Delivery Systems (MSDS) refer to advanced medical devices designed to deliver multiple stents to target locations in vascular treatments. The Peripheral Stent Delivery System focuses on deploying stents in peripheral arteries, addressing conditions like stenosis or occlusions. In contrast, the Vascular Stent Delivery System is utilized for coronary and other vascular structures, offering solutions for atherosclerosis and maintaining vessel patency. Both systems enhance treatment efficacy and patient outcomes by ensuring precise stent placement and reduced procedural time.</p></p>
