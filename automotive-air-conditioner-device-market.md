<p><ul><li>Dependant</li><li>Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air conditioner market is categorized into dependent and independent systems. Dependent systems rely on the vehicle's engine to power the compressor, making them cost-effective but less efficient when the engine is off. Independent systems use a separate power source, such as an electric motor or battery, allowing operation without the engine running, ideal for electric or hybrid vehicles. Both types cater to different vehicle needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and energy consumption in modern automotive designs.</p></p>
