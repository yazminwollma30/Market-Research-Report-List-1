<p><ul><li>CPP Film Line</li><li>CPE Film Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer cast film line market includes distinct segments such as CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film lines and CPE (Cast Polyethylene) film lines. CPP film lines are primarily used for packaging applications due to their excellent clarity, moisture resistance, and sealability, making them ideal for food and industrial packaging. CPE film lines, on the other hand, offer enhanced puncture resistance and flexibility, catering to applications like bags and protective wraps. Both types serve diverse industries, addressing specific packaging needs effectively.</p></p>
