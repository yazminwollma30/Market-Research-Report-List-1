<p><p>The aromatic essential oils market is primarily divided into two types: natural essential oils and synthetic oils. Natural essential oils are derived from plants, flowers, and herbs through processes like steam distillation or cold pressing, preserving their aromatic integrity and therapeutic properties. In contrast, synthetic oils are chemically created to mimic the scent of natural oils, often at a lower cost, but may lack the holistic benefits of their natural counterparts. Both types serve diverse applications in aromatherapy, cosmetics, and perfumery.</p></p>
