<p><p>The interactive video wall market is primarily categorized into three types: LCD, LED, and DLP. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) panels offer high resolution and vibrant colors, making them ideal for indoor settings. LED (Light Emitting Diode) technology provides excellent brightness and energy efficiency, suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. DLP (Digital Light Processing) systems utilize micro-mirror technology for impressive image quality and seamless imagery, often preferred for large-scale displays in professional environments. Each type caters to different visual needs and environments.</p></p>
