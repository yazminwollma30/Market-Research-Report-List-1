<p><p>Pushback racking is a type of storage system designed to optimize warehouse space and increase operational efficiency. It operates on a system of carts that slide back on inclined tracks, allowing pallets to be stored in a dense, compact configuration. When a new pallet is added, the existing ones are pushed back, facilitating easy access and retrieval of goods. This system is particularly beneficial for facilities managing high volumes of similar products, as it maximizes storage density while maintaining swift inventory access.</p><p>The Pushback Racking Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period, driven by the expansion of e-commerce, increasing demand for efficient storage solutions, and the need for effective inventory management. Additionally, technological advancements in racking systems, such as improved materials and designs, are contributing to market growth.
