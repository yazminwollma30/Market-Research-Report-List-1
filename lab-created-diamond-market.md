<p><p>The lab-created diamond market comprises various types based on their growth methods and characteristics. RVD (Revolutionary Vapor Deposition) diamonds are produced using advanced processes that yield high-quality stones. MBD (Microwave Plasma CVD) diamonds utilize microwave technology for efficient growth. SCD (Synthetic Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamonds are created through chemical processes mimicking natural diamond formation. SMD (Synthetic Multi-Crystal Diamonds) involve growing multiple crystals, while DMD (Diamond Micro-Crystal Diamonds) focuses on smaller, industrial-grade diamonds. Each type caters to different consumer needs and applications.</p></p>
