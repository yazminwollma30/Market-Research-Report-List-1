<p><p>The rice steamer market features several types tailored to different consumer needs. Conventional rice cookers use simple on-off heating elements, ideal for basic cooking. Micom rice cookers incorporate microcomputer technology, allowing for precise temperature control and multiple cooking functions, enhancing versatility. In contrast, IH (Induction Heating) rice cookers utilize electromagnetic heating for even cooking and better texture, often featuring advanced options like fuzzy logic. Each type caters to diverse preferences, from simplicity to high-tech culinary experiences.</p></p>
