<p><ul><li>Mechanical Keyboard</li><li>Membrane Keyboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The one-handed keyboard market features two primary types: mechanical and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards utilize individual mechanical switches for each key, providing tactile feedback and durability, making them ideal for gamers and professionals seeking precision. In contrast, membrane keyboards use a pressure-sensitive membrane layer, resulting in a quieter and more lightweight design, appealing to casual users. Both types cater to different preferences, with mechanical keyboards often preferred for performance while membrane keyboards are chosen for comfort and portability.</p></p>
