<p><p>The industrial-grade salt market encompasses various sources, including sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is derived from evaporated seawater and is often purer, making it suitable for various industrial applications. Well and rock salt is mined from underground deposits, ideal for chemical production and de-icing. Lake salt is obtained from evaporated saline lakes and is typically used in industries such as food processing and water treatment. Each type provides unique properties catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
