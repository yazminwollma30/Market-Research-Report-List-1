<p><strong>The Membranes for Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Exchange Membranes</li><li>Ion Exchange Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membranes for water electrolysis are essential components that enhance the efficiency of hydrogen production. Proton Exchange Membranes (PEMs) facilitate the conduction of protons while blocking gases, making them ideal for high-performance applications. They operate at lower temperatures and ensure quicker response times. Ion Exchange Membranes, on the other hand, allow the selective passage of ions and are versatile in different electrolysis setups. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in clean energy technologies and hydrogen production processes.</p></p>
