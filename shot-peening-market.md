<p><p>Shot peening is a surface treatment process used to enhance the mechanical properties of materials by bombarding them with hard, spherical media. This technique induces compressive residual stress on the surface, improving fatigue resistance, corrosion resistance, and overall durability of components. It is widely utilized in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, to optimize performance and prolong the lifespan of critical parts.</p><p>The Shot Peening Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
