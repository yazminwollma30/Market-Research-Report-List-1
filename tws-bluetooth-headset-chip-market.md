Classic Bluetooth chips support continuous high-data-rate streaming suitable for audio applications, ensuring stable connectivity and sound quality. Both chip types cater to different user needs, with Classic chips focusing on audio performance and BLE prioritizing efficiency and longevity in usage.</p></p>
