<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Lithotripter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intracorporeal Lithotripter</li><li>Extracorporeal Lithotripter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Lithotripter market is categorized into two main types: intracorporeal and extracorporeal lithotripters. Intracorporeal lithotripters are used within the body, utilizing ultrasonic waves to fragment stones during minimally invasive procedures, offering precise targeting and reduced trauma. Conversely, extracorporeal lithotripters operate externally, generating shockwaves that travel through the body to disintegrate stones without incisions. Both types aim to enhance patient comfort and surgical outcomes, catering to varying clinical needs and preferences in urology.</p></p>
