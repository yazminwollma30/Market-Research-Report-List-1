<p><p>Metal roof decking is categorized mainly into A roof decks and B roof decks. A roof decks are characterized by their ability to support heavy loads, typically utilized in commercial buildings where structural integrity is paramount. They often incorporate deep ribs to enhance strength and stiffness. B roof decks, on the other hand, are lighter and designed for applications where less load-bearing capacity is required, commonly used in residential or less demanding commercial structures. Both types offer durability and longevity.</p></p>
