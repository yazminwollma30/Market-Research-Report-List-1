<p><p>The bolted bonnet valves market consists of three primary types: bolted bonnet check valves, bolted bonnet globe valves, and bolted bonnet gate valves. Bolted bonnet check valves prevent backflow in piping systems, ensuring fluid flows in one direction. Bolted bonnet globe valves offer precise flow control, ideal for throttling applications. Bolted bonnet gate valves, designed for fully open or closed operations, facilitate minimal flow resistance. These valves are widely used in various industries due to their reliability and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
