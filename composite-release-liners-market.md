<p><p>The composite release liners market consists primarily of two types: paper and film. Release liners made from paper are typically coated with silicone or other release agents, providing a smooth surface ideal for pressure-sensitive adhesives. Film release liners, often made from materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, offer enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and clarity, making them suitable for advanced applications in industries like labels, tapes, and medical devices. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring effective separation and protection in various adhesive products.</p></p>
