<p><strong>The Optical Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Active Chip</li><li>Optical Passive Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical chip market is divided into two main types: optical active chips and optical passive chips. Optical active chips are devices that actively amplify or process signals, such as lasers and modulators, enabling data transmission over long distances. In contrast, optical passive chips do not generate or amplify signals; instead, they manage light signals through components like waveguides and filters. Both types are essential for enhancing communication networks and interconnected systems, driving advancements in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
