<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The press machine market is primarily divided into hydraulic and mechanical types. Hydraulic presses utilize fluid power to generate force, offering precise control and versatility, making them ideal for applications like forming, punching, and stamping. Mechanical presses, on the other hand, rely on a mechanical flywheel or toggle system to deliver force, providing high-speed operations suitable for mass production. Each type serves different industries, with hydraulic presses excelling in complex shapes and mechanical presses favored for high-volume tasks.</p></p>
