<p><strong>The Semiconductor Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Sensors</li><li>PN Junction Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor pressure sensor market features key types such as piezoresistive sensors and PN junction sensors. Piezoresistive sensors operate by changing electrical resistance in response to mechanical stress, making them suitable for precise pressure measurements across various applications. On the other hand, PN junction sensors leverage the voltage changes at a semiconductor junction under pressure variations, offering high accuracy and reliability. Both types are widely used in automotive, industrial, and medical applications, driven by the demand for advanced sensing technologies.</p></p>
