Dual-head pumps have two diaphragms, allowing for higher flow rates and better pressure control, suitable for more demanding tasks. Other configurations may include multi-head designs or specialized variations that cater to unique industrial or medical applications, enhancing versatility and performance in various sectors.</p></p>
