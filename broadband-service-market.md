<p><ul><li>Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line (ADSL)</li><li>FTTX+LAN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The broadband service market encompasses various technologies, including Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line (ADSL) and Fiber-to-the-X (FTTX) combined with Local Area Networks (LAN). ADSL utilizes existing copper telephone lines to provide high-speed internet with lower upload speeds, ideal for residential users. In contrast, FTTX technology leverages fiber-optic connections, offering higher speeds and greater reliability for both residential and commercial applications. When integrated with LANs, FTTX enables efficient data distribution within local networks, enhancing overall performance and connectivity.</p></p>
