<p><p>The Axle Load Sensor market is categorized into four types: </p><p>1. **Spring Mounted** sensors utilize a spring mechanism to measure load variations.  </p><p>2. **Pressure Mounted** sensors rely on pressure transducers to gauge weight.  </p><p>3. **Location Mounted** sensors are placed strategically at specific locations on the axle for precise load measurements.  </p><p>4. **Air Mounted** sensors use pneumatic systems to detect changes in load through air pressure variations.  </p><p>These types cater to different applications across transportation and industrial sectors, enhancing load management and safety.</p></p>
