<p><p>The brass gate valves market can be segmented into two main types based on their end connections: solder end and threaded end. Solder end valves feature a smooth connection that allows for joining with copper pipes using soldering techniques, making them ideal for plumbing applications. Threaded end valves, on the other hand, utilize male and female threads for easy assembly and disassembly, commonly found in industrial settings. Each type caters to specific installation preferences and operational requirements in various industries.</p></p>
