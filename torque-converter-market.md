<p><p>A torque converter is a fluid coupling device used in automatic transmissions to transfer rotating power from an engine to a transmission. It allows the engine to spin independently of the transmission, enabling smooth gear shifts and improved vehicle performance. Torque converters are widely used in automobiles, heavy machinery, and industrial equipment due to their ability to multiply torque and enhance efficiency.</p><p>The Torque Converter Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for automatic transmission vehicles, advancements in automotive technology, and the rise in commercial vehicle production.
