<p><p>The snowboard boots market consists of three main types: front-entry, rear-entry, and side-entry boots. Front-entry boots feature a traditional lace or strap system for a secure fit and easy adjustments, making them popular among traditional riders. Rear-entry boots allow for quick entry and exit, with a hinged design that opens at the back, ideal for beginners. Side-entry boots blend features from both styles, offering customizable fit and comfort by allowing entry from the side, catering to diverse preferences among snowboarders.</p></p>
