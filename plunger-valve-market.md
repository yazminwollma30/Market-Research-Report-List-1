<p><ul><li>Disc Balanced Plunger Valve</li><li>Disc Unbalanced Plunger Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plunger valve market features two main types: disc balanced and disc unbalanced plunger valves. Disc balanced plunger valves utilize a design that evenly distributes pressure across the disc, enhancing stability and reducing wear, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Conversely, disc unbalanced plunger valves do not maintain this equilibrium, which can lead to quicker wear and require more frequent maintenance, often making them more suitable for lower-pressure systems. Both types serve specific operational needs and industries effectively.</p></p>
